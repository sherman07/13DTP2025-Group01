Yearly Member Fee

As a paid member of the Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club, your fees go towards:

✓Insurance of all club assets
✓Maintenance of all club-owned canoes
✓Registration fees and maintenance fees for all club trailers
✓Waka Trolley maintenance
✓Club Running Expenses
✓Affiliation fee for WANZ and AROCA

Starting From

$100

AROCA Fee Structure

AROCA is the regional body for Waka Ama in Auckland, and the AROCA fee is an annual fee payable per paddler per year to this organisation as of October 2016. As an afliated club, it is our responsibility to collect the AROCA fee from each club member and pass this on to AROCA. AROCA fees are below:

  • $10 per Open/Senior (20 years and over)
  • $5 per Junior (14 to 19 years)
  • $5 per Midget/Intermediate (13 years and under)
    •

    WANZ Fee Structure

    WANZ is our governing National Body for Waka Ama, and the WANZ fee is an annual fee payable per paddler per year to this Organisation. As an afliated club, it is our responsibility to collect the WANZ fee from each club member and pass it on to WANZ. WANZ fees are below:

  • $15 per Open/Senior (20 years and over)
  • $12 per Junior (14 to 19 years)
  • $5 per Midget/Intermediate (13 years and under)

    • Additional Costs Not Included

    Please note the following fees are not included in the membership:
    ✘Uniforms
    ✘Regatta Fees
    ✘Canoe Towing Costs
    ✘Personal Paddles

    They are not included in the membership fee and are the responsibility of each individual paddler.

    New Club Members

    Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club (RBOCC) has a trial period for all “new paddlers.” During this period, they have four paddling sessions to decide whether they like the club and the sport of Waka Ama (Outrigger Canoe Paddling) before they are required to join the club to continue paddling.
