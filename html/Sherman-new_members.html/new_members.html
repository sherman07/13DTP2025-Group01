Yearly Member Fee

As a paid member of the Red Beach Outrigger Canoe club, your fees go towards:

✓Insurance of all club assets
✓Maintenance on all club owned canoes
✓Registration fees and maintenance fees of all club trailers
✓Waka Trolley maintenance
✓Club Running Expenses
✓Affiliation fee for WANZ and AROCA

Starting From

$100

AROCA Fee Structure

AROCA is the regional body for Waka Ama in Auckland and the AROCA fee is an annual fee payable per paddler per year to this organisation as of October 2016. As an affiliated club, it is our responsibility to collect the AROCA fee off each club member and pass this onto AROCA. AROCA fees below:

  • $10 per Open/Senior (20 years and over)
  • $5 per Junior (14 to 19 years)
  • $5 per Midget/Intermediate (13 years and under)
    •

    WANZ Fee Structure

    WANZ is our governing National Body for Waka Ama, and the WANZ fee is an annual fee payable per paddler per year to this Organisation. As an affiliated club, it is our responsibility to collect the WANZ fee off each club member and pass it on to WANZ. WANZ fees below:

  • $15 per Open/Senior (20 years and over)
  • $12 per Junior (14 to 19 years)
  • $5 per Midget/Intermediate (13 years and under)

    • Additional Costs Not Included

    Please note the following fees are not included in the membership:
    ✘Uniforms
    ✘Regatta Fees
    ✘Canoe Towing Costs
    ✘Personal Paddles

    They not included in the membership fee and are the responsibility of each individual paddler.

    New Club Members

    Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club (RBOCC) has a trial period for all “new paddlers” where they have 4 paddling sessions to decide whether they like the club and sport of Waka Ama (Outrigger Canoe Paddling) before they are required to join the club to continue paddling.
